Assumptions Used

Simple, explicit inputs. No hand-waving.

  • Ad spend stays fixed at $3K to $6K per month and is optimized for subscriber growth in the USA.
  • Cost-per-subscriber rises over time as the channel scales and audiences saturate.
  • Views scale with subscriber base. Music channels can see wide variance, so three view-velocity scenarios are shown.
  • Ad RPM ranges align with common creator-reported bands. Shorts RPM stays low by design.
  • Non-ad revenue ramps as audience trust grows. Sponsorships and merch matter more by Years 2 to 4.

RPM reference ranges

Long-form creator earnings often land around $2 to $10 per 1,000 views, with many creators clustering near $3 to $5.

Shorts RPM is commonly reported around $0.01 to $0.07 per 1,000 views.

These ranges drive the revenue bands above. Your real outcome depends on US audience mix, content length, and ad demand.
